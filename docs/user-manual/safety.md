# Safety Warnings and Guidelines

⚠️ **IMPORTANT**: Please read and follow all safety warnings and guidelines before operating your Malyan M200 Ultra 3D printer.

## General Safety Warnings

### Installation and Setup

- **Indoor Use Only**: This device is intended for indoor use only. Do not install this device on an unstable surface where it could fall and cause either personal injury or damage to the device. Besides, it will affect printing results.

- **Unpacking**: Do not force or tear anything when during unpacking and setup. This may cause damage to the printer and/or its accessories.

### Power and Electrical Safety

- **Inspection**: Prior to operation, check the unit and power cord for physical damage. Do not use if physical damage has occurred.

- **Power Requirements**: Ensure that the outlet provides the same type and level of power required by the device (110V/220V).

- **USB Connection**: Do not remove or disconnect the USB cable when printing from a computer.

- **Power Cord Safety**: 
  - Take care to prevent damage to the power cord
  - Do not allow it to become crimped, pinched, walked on, or become tangled with other cords
  - Ensure that the power cord does not present a tripping hazard

- **Unplugging**: Unplug this device from the power source when not in use. Never unplug the unit by pulling on the power cord. Always grasp the connector head or adapter body.

- **Service and Repairs**: Ensure that the printer is turned off and unplugged from its power source before making repairs or performing service.

### Hot Surfaces Warning

⚠️ **HIGH TEMPERATURE HAZARD**

- **Hot Parts**: Take care to avoid touching hot parts, including:
  - Heat blocks
  - Extruder nozzle
  - Extruded filament
  - The heated build plate

- **Cooling Period**: Do not reach inside the printer during operation. Always allow the printer and extruded filament to cool before reaching inside.

### Environmental Protection

- **Extreme Conditions**: Do not subject the product to extreme force, shock, or fluctuations in temperature or humidity.

- **Moisture Protection**: If moisture does get in or on the device, immediately unplug it from the power outlet and allow it to fully dry before reapplying power.

## Operating Safety Guidelines

### During Printing

1. **Supervision**: Always supervise prints, especially during the first few layers
2. **Ventilation**: Ensure adequate ventilation when printing with materials that produce fumes (PETG)
3. **First Layer Watch**: Monitor the first layer closely to ensure proper adhesion
4. **No Interference**: Do not interfere with the printer while it is operating

### Material Handling

1. **Filament Storage**: Store filament in dry, sealed containers to prevent moisture absorption
2. **Material Compatibility**: Only use materials compatible with your printer (PLA, PETG)
3. **Temperature Settings**: Always use appropriate temperature settings for your material
4. **Clean Nozzle**: Keep the nozzle clean to prevent clogs and poor print quality

### Maintenance Safety

1. **Power Off**: Always turn off and unplug the printer before maintenance
2. **Cool Down**: Allow all components to cool before touching or cleaning
3. **Sharp Tools**: Use caution when using scrapers or cutting tools near the build plate
4. **Moving Parts**: Be aware of moving parts and belt tension when inspecting

## Workspace Safety

### Setup Location

- **Stable Surface**: Place on a stable, level surface
- **Ventilation**: Ensure adequate airflow around the printer
- **Clearance**: Maintain clearance for moving parts (X, Y, Z axes)
- **Fire Safety**: Keep flammable materials away from the printer
- **Child Safety**: Keep out of reach of children and pets

### Emergency Procedures

### Emergency Stop

- **Power Switch**: Immediately turn off the power switch in case of emergency
- **Unplug**: Unplug from power outlet if safe to do so
- **Cool Down**: Allow hot components to cool before inspection

### Fire Safety

- **Fire Extinguisher**: Keep a suitable fire extinguisher nearby (Class A, B, C)
- **Smoke Detector**: Ensure smoke detectors are functional in the printing area


## Compliance and Certification

- **CE Marking**: This device complies with CE safety standards
- **FCC Compliance**: Meets FCC regulations (where applicable)
